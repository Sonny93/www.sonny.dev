Finding the right host for your project's needs can sometimes be tricky, so here's a very simple guide to help you make that choice.

## Where to start?

It seems obvious to start this guide with the most important criterion: **price**.

- Price
- Desired performance
- Storage capacity
- Hosting duration (discounts possible for longer terms)

## When you have a budget!

I **recommend** prioritizing _"paid" hosts_ which, as you might expect, generally _offer_ a **_quality of service_** and **_uptime_** noticeably **_better_** than free hosts.

- [Contabo](https://contabo.com/en)
- [Hetzner](https://www.hetzner.com/)
- [OVH](https://www.ovhcloud.com/en/)
- [AWS](https://aws.amazon.com/)

## We love it when it's free!

That said, like any good developer, I have plenty of side projects and really don't want to pay for hosting on each one, so here's the list of free services I use.

- [Github Pages](https://pages.github.com/)
- [Vercel](https://vercel.com/)
- [Netlify](https://www.netlify.com/)
- [Alwaysdata](https://www.alwaysdata.com/en/)
- [AWS](https://aws.amazon.com/free/) offers a free tier valid for one year from first use.
